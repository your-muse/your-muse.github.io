Sputnik, My Sweetheart

Meet me at our supercuts
I've begun imagining you in places you never were
my mouth spills the words I never told you
my skin absorbs the ones I did
letting the horror remain within myself
before I corrupt you with the version of myself I never wanted the sun to see.

I hate existing in the pain of the past
every moment I think of you — a hellish loop
it presents itself in the quiet
a man at my door
a shadow on my chest
a figure at my feet
they all carry the vengeance that you did.

Your palms never reach for mine again
spare the rod, spoil the child.

I’m merging with you
your soul ripping shenanigans finding a host in me
before I know it, I start seeing you in the looking glass before I see myself.

You glare at the mirror
as if it's the sight of you that made it crack.

Sumire stirs in her embrace
Miu does the honour of holding her
she cries for a Miu she never saw
come the moon-rise, they won't be together anymore
from the coast of Japan to the shores of Athens
the expanse of our world is devoid of her scatterbrained stories & cigarette laden breath.

Wake me up when we’re under the same moon again.

~by Simantini
